How to get
your YouTube export

Step I

Visit Google Takeout

Go to takeout.google.com and sign in with your Google account. Click "Deselect all" to start fresh, then scroll to find "YouTube and YouTube Music."

Step II

Select history only

Inside the YouTube option, click "All YouTube data included" and deselect everything except "history." This keeps the file small and focused on watch data.

Step III

Upload the file here

Download the export ZIP, unzip it, and locate watch-history.html or watch-history.json inside. Drag it onto the field above.
