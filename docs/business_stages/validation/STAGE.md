# Validation Stage — ViewPulse (Historical)

**Note:** ViewPulse was built without following the Startup Factory framework. This documents what Validation would look like if done formally.

---

## Validation Hypothesis

Users will upload their watch history if:
1. Privacy is guaranteed (no server, all local)
2. Value is immediate (clear insights from first upload)
3. It's free (no payment barrier)

## What Actually Happened

### Validation Signals (Retrospective)
- ✅ Early adopters used it and shared results
- ✅ Reddit posts got positive reception
- ✅ No complaints about privacy
- ❌ No formal waitlist
- ❌ No systematic user interviews

### What We'd Do Differently
1. **Formal waitlist** — Capture emails before full launch
2. **User interviews** — 10+ interviews with early users
3. **Influencer outreach** — Privacy YouTubers, tech reviewers
4. **Landing page A/B test** — Different value props

## Validation Artifacts (Missing)

1. ❌ User Stories (8 detailed personas)
2. ❌ Product Design Document
3. ❌ Waitlist of 100 people
4. ❌ 10 influencers identified

## Lessons for Future Projects

- Build waitlist BEFORE launching
- Interview users immediately after first use
- Document all validation evidence
- Never skip this stage even for "obvious" products
